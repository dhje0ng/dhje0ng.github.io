Summary

On 10 August I gave a presentation on ‘Automotive USB Fuzzing’ at the Car Hacking Village at DEFCON in Las Vegas.

The above topic is a presentation that describes the process of discovering USB vulnerabilities in real-world vehicles, including the CVE-2022-39075 and CVE-2022-39076 vulnerabilities, and how to effectively perform USB fuzzing in the vehicles we studied.
